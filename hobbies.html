crocheting

over the summers spent in taiwan with nothing to do, i picked up crocheting from my grandma. we would spend days and nights in the living room under the noisy fan, crocheting together in comfortable silence, once in awhile a question being asked or a piece of advice being given. my passion for crocheting soon extended beyond a way to kill time, and i started making crafts for fun. from bags to tank tops to sweaters, from online patterns to self-made ones, i have created 15 and counting pieces of work. it's a way to get my creativity flowing and also a way of relieving everyday stress, and it always gives me insurmountable pride to see something i physically created with my hands. click here to see my best works!
