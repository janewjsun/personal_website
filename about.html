get to know me!

personal

My name is Jane Sun, and I am a soon-to-be 20-year-old, originally from the Bay Area. My nuclear family consists of my mom Cindy, teenage brother Jacob, and dad Zheng, as pictured. As a family, we love to travel and immerse ourselves in local culture, watch action movies, discuss politics and current events, and take relaxing strolls. We moved around a lot throughout my childhood and eventually settled down in Beijing. I grew up in an international community and had the pleasure of meeting people from all over the world, allowing me to gain a lot of perspective and empathy.

Other than being a student, I am an avid crocheter and reader. Since the age of 5, I've created dozens of works, following my grandma's instructions, online tutorials, and more recently my own patterns. It started off as curiosity, but this hobby has thus turned into a way for me to tap into my creativity and produce something physical. I have been a huge bookworm since kindergarden, taking a brief break in my underclassmen years of high school but picking it back up during the pandemic. In 2022, I read 38, and I am aiming for 40 in 2023! My favorite genres are definitely mystery and romance, but I do enjoy a good science fiction or memoir every once in awhile.

professional

Currently, I am interested in program design and web development.

In terms of programming skills, I have 2 years of experience with Python and 1.5 with C++, at an intermediate-to-advanced level of understanding with both. Through self-learning via projects, I have also had exposure to HTML, CSS, and Javascript, along with APIs associated with Javascript, such as React or Node. These skills are still rapidly improving. My preferred languages remain to be Python and C++.
